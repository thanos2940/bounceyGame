BOUNCEY

Bouncey is a fun and challenging game!
Challenge yourself to see how long you can survive and achieve highscores.

You got 3 lives to prove yourself worthy. The more the ball bounces, the faster it goes!
For every 1000 points that you score, you progress through a level.
At level 3 and up the game becomes more and more challenging!

Enjoy the adrenaline ride and conquer the levels!

Use A and D to move the platform around.
